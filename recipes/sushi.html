Sarah's Sushi Bake

Description: Budget friendly, healthy and tastes just like sushi.

Based on multiple recipes but specifically the NYT one

    For the sauce:
  • 1 can of Ocean's salmon
  • 1 teaspoon of soy sauce
  • Srirachia to taste, depending on your spice level
  • Kewpie mayo
  • 1 cup dry of sushi rice
  • Seaweed
  • Sesame seeds
    Steps
  1. Cook sushi rice fully
  2. Add 1/4 of a cup of sushi rice to a cold stainless steel pan, and mash in the canned salmon with the juices
  3. Add drizzle of mayo and hot sauce to your liking, with a teaspoon of soy sauce (the fish is already salty so you can't add too much)
  4. Put whole pan in oven at 350 for 10-15 mins, until its a bit browned
  5. Serve and garnish with extra mayo, hot sauce, and sesame seeds, eat by putting the rice and salmon seaweed, lightly dip in soy sauce and enjoy!
