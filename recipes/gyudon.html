Easy Gyudon

Description: Delicious, sweet, and savory gyudon. Best made the day before eating.

  • 1/2 bl of Thinly sliced Beef (200~250g) fatty beef is better
  • 1 Onion
  • 1 pc ginger (5g)
  • Cooked rice
  • 2 cups water
  • 2 tbsp mirin
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 3 tbsp sake
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • dashi powder
    Steps
  1. Slice onions/ginger thinly
  2. Combine all sauces/sugar in a pot, then add meat the pot
  3. Simmer/lightly boil on medium for 5 mins until foam appears and skim off foam
  4. Add thinly sliced ginger and half the onions
  5. Reduce heat to medium low and cook for 20 mins
  6. Add the other half of the onion and cook until desired doneness
